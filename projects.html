Music Interval Trainer

A single-page HTML app that teaches and drills musical interval recognition using a quiz interface. The craziest thing about this began when I dusted off some old code of my own for a few new requirements and responsiveness updates. I estimated a week or so (depending on how many sunny water days were coming up), decided to fully refactor it, and since I was biting that bullet, decided to see what Google Gemini could do with the total requirements, especially a smart "drill" algorithm to repeat missed questions, more often but still at random. Here's what it did.

In about 90 minutes. Most of that being follow up tweaks and bugsmashing, all done with refining prompts.Yikes! Time to go kayaking...
